Sponsor a Child

At Josephine Patrick Foundation Uganda, your support provides more than just basic needs—it gives a child a second chance at love, family, and a hopeful future. Whether you choose to give once or become a monthly donor, every contribution plays a vital role in changing lives.

Your donation helps us provide loving homes for children who have been abandoned or are at risk of losing parental care. With your support, we can offer them food, shelter, medical care, and emotional support in a safe and nurturing environment. You also help fund educational programs, from early childhood learning to vocational training, equipping young people with the tools they need to succeed and break the cycle of poverty.

When you give to Josephine Patrick Foundation, you are not just making a donation—you are investing in the future of children and their communities. Your generosity creates lasting change, offering hope and new opportunities to those who need it most.
